GeoGebra thumbnails: bookmarklet

The GeoGebra forums have a little problem: You have to launch GeoGebra to see the .GGB worksheets attached to messages. That's a drag if you're using a slow computer.

This page contains something to alleviate the problem: a bookmarklet[?] to show you the worksheets' thumbnails (and construction protocol). Drag the following link to your browser's toolbar (or bookmark it with your right mouse button). Then, when you're browsing a forum page, click the button to show all the thumbnails.
